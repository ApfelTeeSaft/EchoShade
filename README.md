# EchoShade
> **Real-time defensive audio perturbation — protect your voice from unauthorized AI cloning.**

EchoShade applies imperceptible adversarial perturbations to your microphone stream and audio files,
destabilizing speaker embeddings used by voice cloning systems (VALL-E, RVC, So-VITS-SVC, ElevenLabs, etc.)
while preserving perceptual quality for human listeners.

Inspired by [Glaze](https://glaze.cs.uchicago.edu/) and [AntiFake](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.09040),
but built for **real-time audio** with platform-aware codec survivability.
